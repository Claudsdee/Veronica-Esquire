+ Artist of the Month +

A hip hop music producer from Brooklyn, New York. Starting rapping when he was 13 years old. Graduated 3 years ago from college and is now a full time producer.

The Phronetic has honed in on his craft and explains concepts and ideas to his growing fan base with humility when asked

His name The Phronetic comes from the ancient Greek word "Phronesis" , which is a term for "practical wisdom". The Phronetic mentioned, "It is a state of being where one knows how to act with virtue in practical, every day situations." He also mentioned in an interview aricle "I always strive to reach that state of being so I decided to call myself the Phronetic to symbolize my journey to Phronesis."
